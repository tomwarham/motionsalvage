About Motion Salvage

Motion Salvage are the top choice in the North West for removing scrap / unwanted cars. All of our team of staff have years of experience in all aspects of motor mechanics, transport and salvage.

We carry out all aspects of motor vehicle recycling and removal, some of which are: General vehicle removal, scrap car disposal / recycling, auto wreckage recovery, car recycling, auto salvage and breaking. If you are unsure of what auto service you are looking for or want to chat about a removal, feel free to contact us.

We are a licensed vehicle salvage company (Licence : CB/lm3486tt) and our car scrapping/recycling process meets all environmental recycling requirements. On top of this we make sure any car parts outside of the recycling requirements are also recycled as much as possible .

To collect your vehicle, all we need is the logbook (v5c document). We will complete the required sections of your logbook when we come to collect your vehicle.
